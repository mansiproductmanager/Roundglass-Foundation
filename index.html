Bringing Wholistic Wellbeing to Communities

The Roundglass Foundation (RGF Global) is on a mission to develop, replicate, and scale a development model for the under-served and marginalized rural communities. Our unique approach focuses on **Wholistic Wellbeing** by enabling people to take control of their own destiny through co-owned and co-created thematic interventions in areas critical for a better future, primarily in Punjab, India.

We strive to create a thriving ecosystem for rural growth, encompassing the wellbeing of the individual, society, and the planet.

Our Thematic Areas of Impact

Environment and Sustainability (Sustain Punjab)

Mobilizing communities to restore their environment through projects like The Billion Tree Project (reforestation with native species), Waste Management programs for a cleaner society, and the promotion of Regenerative Agriculture practices.

Youth Development (Learn Punjab)

Creating global citizens of tomorrow by fostering curiosity, cognitive flexibility, and critical thinking. This includes experiential learning solutions (Learn Labs) and using sports as a medium to nurture potential for leadership and teamwork (Sports Centers, 1Girl1Football Program).

Women's Equity (Her Punjab)

Breaking the transgenerational cycle of bias by facilitating social and economic empowerment. Programs focus on skill training, entrepreneurial aptitude, creating market linkages (Livelihood Programs), and health/wellbeing awareness workshops.

Our Impact At a Glance

In just a few years, our Model Village initiatives have reached a significant number of villages and lives.

  • **Over 3.5 Million** Native trees planted.
  • **Hundreds** of Waste Management projects implemented.
  • **Hundreds** of Learn Labs and Sports Centers engaging thousands of children.
  • **Thousands** of women and adolescent girls reached with health and livelihood workshops.
